About

Hi, I am Sparsh Paliwal. Computer Science, as a subject, is something that has intrigued me right from my school days. I choose Computer Science as an elective in high school. Since I have always enjoyed acquiring information, learning new concepts and ideas that the courses introduced me to, and stood amongst the best performers in the subject.

I decided to pursue my undergraduate degree in the field of Information Technology at National Institute of Technology, Durgapur which is among the most reputed Government Institutions in India. Since childhood, I have pursued art for recreation. This meant taking part in various competitions and workshops during school and college. I was the Vice President of Dramatics club at NIT, Durgapur. We won two national level dramatics competitions for street plays, won a competition in mono-acting. I wrote and directed a street play. This experience has educated me a lot about teamwork and leadership. I was involved in fine arts since my school days but in college, I realized my love towards digital art while working on different art projects like designing posters for events, short films, and assets for mobile games. By leveraging the knowledge in both arts (specifically digital) and technology I created and published many application.

One of the life-changing experience was the Google Summer of Code internship where I created a game named Discovery Cat. This was my first encounter with the full product development cycle. The game was special because it was accessible by people irrespective of their age or disabilities(Inclusive design) and helped them discover their digital and learning preferences. Apart from this, I have completed numerous personal projects which can be found on Github. Also, I open sourced one of my personal projects to teach open source to college students, eventually developing a chrome extension which now has around 600 downloads. To put in simple words, I love application development.
